树形背包

\(f_{i,j}\)表示\(i\)子树\(j\)容量

\(g_{i,j}\)表示不选\(i\)的子树\(j\)容量

初始\(g_{i,j}=0\)

如果选\(i\)就一定选整个子树 ：\(max(g_{i,j},\sum v_i)(j \ge \sum v_i)\)

\(g'_{i,a+b}=g_{i,a}+f_{c,b}\)

\[ f_{i,j}=\left\{\begin{array}l g_{i,j} & j>\sum v_i,\\ max(g_{i,j}, \sum v_i) & j\ge \sum v_i. \end{array}\right. \]

时间复杂度\(O(n^3)\)
